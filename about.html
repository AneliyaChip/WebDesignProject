Our Story and Values

Walking Adventure is a friendly outdoor company that organises safe, enjoyable, and accessible walking experiences for people of all ages and abilities. Our goal is to help customers connect with nature, explore new places, and enjoy the positive benefits of walking in the fresh air. We believe that walking brings confidence, well-being, and a sense of community. We plan our guided routes with these values in mind. We run our activities on a volunteer basis. Through our website, you can organise school groups or coworker groups. You can also request one of our trained hikers to show you the local nature and heritage. Since our guides are volunteers, you will need to fit their availability. Their expertise is worth it. We wish you wonderful and peaceful adventure experiences.
